3 Data We Collect

Account & authentication data:

  • Phone number and phone verification status
  • Optional full name and optional email address
  • Password hash (never stored in plain text)
  • OTP verification metadata and refresh-session metadata
  • Device ID / device name, IP address, user agent

Order & subscription data:

  • Meal selections, custom meal / salad selections
  • Subscription plans and days, pickup / delivery status
  • Delivery zone and address (where delivery is used)
  • Promo code usage and operational history

Payment data:

  • Payment provider identifiers, invoice and payment status
  • Payment amounts, redirect / callback metadata
  • Moyasar webhook data
💳

basicdite does not store full payment card details on its own servers. Card data is handled by Moyasar, our payment provider.

Push notification data:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) device tokens when you enable notifications

Logs & security data:

  • Server logs, request metadata, webhook processing data
  • Rate-limit events and audit / activity logs
  • Log redaction is applied to sensitive fields

4 Why We Collect Data

  • Create, authenticate, and secure user accounts
  • Verify phone numbers; support login, password reset, and session refresh
  • Process orders, subscriptions, pickup, delivery, and customer support workflows
  • Process and reconcile payments through Moyasar
  • Send OTP codes via Twilio and push notifications via Firebase
  • Fraud prevention, abuse prevention, system integrity, and legal compliance
  • Operate admin, kitchen, courier, and reporting workflows

5 How Data Is Stored & Secured

  • Data is stored in MongoDB
  • Passwords are stored as cryptographic hashes — never in plain text
  • Refresh tokens are stored as HMAC hashes — not as raw tokens
  • All secrets are loaded from environment variables — not hard-coded
  • All data in transit is protected by HTTPS / TLS
  • Sensitive log fields are redacted before writing to logs
🛡️

The backend also uses authentication middleware, rate limiting on sensitive endpoints, CORS allowlists, security headers, and structured webhook validation.

6 Third-Party Service Providers

We share data only with the following service providers, strictly to operate basicdite:

  • Twilio — OTP delivery via SMS or WhatsApp for phone verification
  • Moyasar — Payment invoice processing and webhook reconciliation
  • Firebase (Google) — Cloud Messaging for push notifications
  • Hosting & infrastructure providers — Server hosting, database, logging, and backup services used to operate and maintain the platform
🚫

We do not sell personal data. We do not share data for advertising, marketing, or analytics with any third party.

7 Authentication & Account Data

Users authenticate using phone-based OTP and/or a password, depending on the app flow. Twilio sends or verifies login codes. The backend stores phone numbers, optional names and email addresses, password hashes, refresh-session records, and device / push tokens.

Inactive or deleted accounts are blocked from login and protected API access immediately upon deletion.

8 Analytics & Crash Reporting

basicdite does not integrate any analytics SDKs or crash-reporting SDKs into the app. Server logs may collect operational and error metadata necessary to run, debug, secure, and protect the service. These logs are not used for profiling, behavioral advertising, or sharing with ad networks.

9 Push Notifications

If you grant notification permission, basicdite registers a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token on our server. This token is used solely to deliver order status and service notifications. You can revoke notification permission at any time from your device settings, and we will stop sending push notifications.

10 Payments

Payments are processed through Moyasar. We store payment identifiers, status, amounts, and webhook / reconciliation metadata. Payment and order records are retained as required for accounting, tax, fraud prevention, refunds, dispute handling, and legal obligations.

11 Data Retention

Account profile data is retained while the account is active. Upon account deletion, sessions are revoked, password hashes and push tokens are removed, and login is disabled.

Account profile (active accounts) Duration of account
Account deletion & security records Up to 90 days
Server logs Up to 30 days
Database backups (may contain deleted data) Up to 30 days
Support records Up to 180 days after last interaction
Orders & payment records As required by law / accounting

12 Your Rights & Account Deletion

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may request account deletion in two ways:

  • In-app: Authenticated deletion via the app — processed immediately by soft deletion.
  • Online form: Submit a request at basicdiet145.onrender.com/account-deletion — requires manual verification before any data is changed.

The online form collects your email address, an optional reason, and confirmation. For other privacy requests (access, correction, or general support), contact us at the email below.
