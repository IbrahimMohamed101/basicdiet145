basicdite Account Deletion

Use this page to request deletion of your basicdite account. You can also request deletion from inside the app when signed in.

Authenticated in-app deletion disables the account, revokes active sessions, removes push notification tokens, and prevents login. Deleted account personal profile data is deleted or anonymized where technically and legally possible. Account deletion requests may be retained for up to 90 days, server logs for up to 30 days, backups for up to 30 days before overwrite, and support records for up to 180 days after the last interaction. Order and payment records may be retained where required for accounting, tax, fraud prevention, refunds, dispute handling, and legal obligations.
